Bacon Recipe

Description

Easy recipe for crispy and tasty bacon.

Ingredients

  • Favorite store bought bacon(however maany pieces)
  • A pan

Directions

  1. Open package of bacon and take out however many pieces you would like.
  2. Throw the strips of bacon on the pan, the pan should be on low heat.
  3. Cook bacon until desired crisp and make sure to throw away the grease!
