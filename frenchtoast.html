French Toast Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for delicious, fluffy, and easy to make french toast.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Slices of brioche bread
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • Optional:syrup

Directions

  1. Mix together 1 egg, 1 tsp of vanilla, and 1 tsp of cinnamon.
  2. Dip both sides of bread in mixture until well soaked.
  3. Put slices of bread on lightly greased pan and cook unitl golden brown.
  4. Top with favorite toppings and enjoy.
