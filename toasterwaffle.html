Toast Waffle Recipe

Description

Recipe for the best toaster waffles.

Ingredients

  • Favorite brand of toaster waffles
  • Butter
  • Whatever toppings you enjoy(syrup, powdered sugar, berries, etc.)

Directions

  1. Pop however many waffles you want into toaster.
  2. Toast until waffles are slightly golden and lightly toasted for best texture.
  3. Take waffles out of toaster and butter them up.
  4. Add favorite toppings and enjoy!
